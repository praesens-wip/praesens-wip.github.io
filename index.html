Welcome to Präsens

At Präsens, we slow things down. We take a step back from the fast-paced energy that often dominates music spaces, and create something more grounded, more present. In a landscape where musical experiences often chase constant intensity, instant gratification and are built around constant stimulation, we’re choosing a different path. One that values depth over speed, emotion over impact, and the power of listening over performance. We create spaces where the music itself becomes the main act, and where presence means more than just being there. One that’s less about escaping the everyday and more about being fully there for it.

Driven by our love for music across all genres—be it jazz, ambient, soul, funk, or electronic—we began crafting our own sound systems and curating spaces where sound can be experienced in unexpected ways. Whether it’s a dedicated listening session, a Sunday hangout, or a spontaneous gathering by the water, we create moments where music connects, resonates, and inspires.

Präsens is not about exclusivity—it’s about openness. A welcoming invitation to anyone curious enough to let the music in. We believe in building a community where everyone feels like an essential part of something meaningful. Where people are not just guests, but co-creators of the atmosphere. You don’t need to know the artists or the genre. You just need to show up and let yourself be part of a shared moment. Whether you're lying on a carpet, sitting in a corner, or swaying with your eyes closed—there’s no right way to listen. Just your way.

We see Präsens as a bridge: between genres, between people, between sound and emotion. And we’re excited to share this journey with all who feel drawn to explore music in a more conscious, connected way.
